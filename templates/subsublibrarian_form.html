And, finally, input what you want to search for. Regexes are supported and on by default.


Regexes, or regular expressions, let you do some cool stuff with searching. So, for example, searching for " (m|f)(o|a)ther " will get a hit on anything with " mother " or " father " - or " mather " or " fother ", for that matter. See the link above for more fun things you can do with regexes.
